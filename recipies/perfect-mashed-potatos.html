Perfect Mashed Potatos

Description

These mashed potatoes are perfect for any occasion. They are creamy, buttery, and delicious. They are easy to make and are sure to be a hit with your family and friends.

Ingredients

  1. THree large russet ptoatoes, peeled and cut in half lengthwise
  2. 1/2 cup whole milk
  3. 1/4 cup butter
  4. Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Place pototoes in a large pot and cover with saltered water. Bring to a boil. Reduce het to medium-low, cover, and simmer until tender, 20 to 25 minutes

  2. Drain potatoes, then retun to the pot.. Turn heat to high and allow potatoes to dry for about 30 seconds. Turn off heat.

  3. Mash potatoes with a potato msuher twice around the pot, then add milk and butter. Continue to mash utnil smooth and fluffy. Wisk in salt and black perrer until evenly distributed, about 15 seconds
